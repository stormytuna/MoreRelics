[h2]TLDR[/h2]
- Adds 24 new relics
  - 2 Common
  - 7 Uncommon
  - 4 Rare
  - 7 Boss
  - 4 Shop
- Adds relics to Defect's and Watcher's pool to match the number that Ironclad and Silent have
- Configuration to disable each relic individually
